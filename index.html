Brand Story

▍”The poop-picker truck, the advanced technology, is a universal labor vehicle of slaves”.

World premiere! My cat lover, my proud artistic toys - “Extreme Masochism! Cat keeper”! As senior cat servants who love toys 虎豹屋敷 UltraTigerToys team members have created “Extreme M! Cat-keeper” combined artistic toys.

The name of the brand “虎豹屋敷（Ultra Tigar Toys)” is an honorary title for cats, that is treating cats as majestic as tigers and leopards; “屋敷 is Yashiki (やしき)” which means “a big house with servants” which is derived from Japan, and the servants of cats are “cat lovers”; however, those servants actually have high social status in the world, but they are still willing to serve their pets for anything which mean is those people are real cat lovers. All in all, this is a kind of subculture with humor that symbolize a hierarchical society in this generation.
